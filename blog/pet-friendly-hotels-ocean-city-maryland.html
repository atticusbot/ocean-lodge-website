Pet Friendly Hotels in Ocean City Maryland: What to Know Before You Book

By Ocean Lodge Ocean City  ·  Ocean City, MD  ·  March 2026

Finding pet friendly hotels in Ocean City Maryland is harder than it should be. Beach towns are a natural destination for people who travel with dogs — but most Ocean City hotels restrict or ban pets outright. The ones that do allow pets often bury the caveats: size limits, breed restrictions, extra fees that surface at checkout. This guide covers what to actually look for, and where Mallard Apartments at Ocean Lodge fits in.

Why Pet-Friendly Options Are Scarce in Ocean City

Ocean City is a high-density, high-turnover beach destination. Most hotels turn rooms quickly in summer, and many properties have decided the liability and turnover cost of allowing pets isn't worth it. The result: a genuine shortage of quality, centrally-located pet-friendly accommodations.

What you're more likely to find when searching are:

  • Properties that allow pets in name only, with so many restrictions they're effectively unusable
  • Vacation rentals that are pet-friendly but far from the beach or boardwalk
  • Hotels with pet fees that weren't disclosed upfront

The practical ask when traveling with a dog is pretty simple: a place where you can come and go without disruption, where your pet isn't treated like a liability, and where the setup makes the trip easier — not harder.

Tips for Bringing Your Pet to Ocean City MD

Even with the right accommodations, there are a few things worth knowing before you arrive:

  • Beach access rules vary by season. Ocean City has restrictions on when and where dogs are allowed on the beach. Rules typically allow pets during early morning and evening hours in summer. Check current City of Ocean City ordinances before your trip.
  • The boardwalk has restrictions too. During peak season, pets are generally not allowed on the boardwalk during daytime hours. Plan morning or off-hours walks if that's part of your itinerary.
  • Bring what you need. Having a full kitchen at Mallard means you can manage your pet's food and feeding schedule the same way you would at home — no hunting for a pet store mid-trip.
  • Book early. Pet-friendly inventory is limited. Summer dates fill faster than most people expect.
